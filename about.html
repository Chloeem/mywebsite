Know me.

My Story:

A self-taught Graphic Designer. Before corona hit, I was self-employed and working as a chef in a vegan restaurant, whilst following autodidact study of programming in HTML/CSS/JS.

I aim to progress to full-stack development so that I can offer digital architecture from the ground up and be location independent again. The dream would be to split my workload between sustainable/eco corporate work with a human-focused approach and building for NGO's or grass-roots ventures alongside.

My ultimate goal is to be able to travel to anywhere in the world and offer my help or teachings to people in need. I want to use my privileged position to learn as much as possible and help make a positive change in the world.
